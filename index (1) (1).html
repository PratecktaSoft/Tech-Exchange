We accept virtually all brands and models for exchange: iPhone (all models), Samsung, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Vivo, Oppo, Pixel, and more. The condition affects the valuation but never the ability to exchange.

We evaluate based on model, storage capacity, physical condition, battery health, and live market rates updated daily. Our appraisers are trained to give you the highest fair market value. The evaluation is completely free and there's no obligation to sell. If you have a better offer from another dealer, show us — we'll often beat it. We don't believe in low-balling; our volume means we can offer you more.

All repairs come with a minimum 90-day warranty on parts and labour. Most repairs — including screen replacements on flagship iPhones and MacBook keyboard replacements — come with a full 6-month warranty. If anything fails within the warranty period due to our repair, we fix it completely free of charge. You'll receive a written warranty certificate with every repair.

Your data security is our top priority. Our technicians are strictly prohibited from accessing any personal data on your device. We've never had a data breach or complaint in 7 years of operation.
